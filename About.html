NIJISANJI EN

NIJISANJI EN is an overseas branch NIJISANJI PROJECT, with english-speaking livers. It is not aimed for a specific country rather, it is aimed for English-speaking audiences world wide. There are different units of livers, all with different and unique talents and characteristics.

HISTORY

On June 22 2020, Ichikara Inc. rebranded Nijisanji IN to Nijisanji EN. On December 1 2020, It was stated that the official launch of preliminary auditions for Nijisanji. Ongoing auditions ended on December 15th.1
